Who are we ?

We are Breath dynamics International. (abbreviated BDI.) Our goal is to create relationships between international investors and the Companies or African governments that can help facilitate the transfer of technology and capital as well as that the use of business to inspire and implement solutions that respect to promote Africa's sustainable development. Thanks to our relationships and in our experience, we hope to combat poverty and immigration of young Africans to the West by bringing job-creating projects to our countries.

"Empowering Africa: BDI's Strategic Investments for a Brighter Future"

With our ambition to contribute our stone to the construction and development of Africa, we continuously invest in the quality of our service delivery. In addition to upgrading our investors, BDI. focuses on development of relationships. Our global ambitions also extend to our geographical presence in Africa. with the early opening of regional offices on the African continent. Shaping a Sustainable Future: From Housing to Infrastructure

We aim for a sustainable future with affordable housing projects, renewable energy, bridging the digital divide, a sustainable fishing industry, responsible solid waste management, and the development of solid infrastructure such as roads, bridges and highways.

Affordable Housing Project

Affordable housing in our country is pivotal for low to moderate-income families, requiring a collaborative financing process and government support to yield significant national benefits and inspire innovative design solutions.

Renewable Project

Embracing renewable energy is our pathway to the future, enabling us to power our lives sustainably, while innovative designs for affordable housing provide a foundation for a thriving community.

No more digital divide

Leveraging optical fiber technology for digital aperture, we aim to enhance access to knowledge, accelerate digital development, and strive to reduce the digital divide.

Solid Waste Management

In waste management, we engage in the collection, trading, transportation, treatment, reuse, and disposal of waste, primarily from human activities, to mitigate its impact on human and environmental health.

Roads Bridges Highways

Just as veins are vital for our body's vitality by circulating nutrients, roads and highways are crucial for our economy, enabling the flow of goods and keeping our economy thriving.

Fishing Industries

At BDI, we leverage strategic partnerships to secure large fishing boats for countries and corporations, thereby driving the growth of the industrial fishing industry.
